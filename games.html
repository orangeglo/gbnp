  • Kirby's Dream Land 2
  • Mario Picross 2
  • Mega Man IV & V
  • Mole Mania
  • Pokemon Red/Green (JP Only)
  • Super Mario Land 2 & 3
  • Tetris DX
  • Zelda: Link's Awakening (Not DX)

2 Blocks (256KB)

  • Bionic Commando
  • Kid Dracula
  • Kirby's Dream Land
  • Mario's Picross
  • Mega Man I, II, & III
  • Metroid II: Return of Samus
  • Trip World

  • Cave Noire
  • Dr. Mario
  • F-1 Race
  • Gargoyle's Quest
  • Solar Striker
  • Super Mario Land
  • Tetris
